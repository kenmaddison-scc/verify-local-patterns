Get help transferring photo

Memory card

A memory card is a small plastic card used as removable storage for your photos. Your camera automatically saves your photos to your memory card.

Take the memory card out of your camera and insert it into your computer. Your photos are now on your computer.

Transfer cable

A transfer cable is a lead which can connect your camera to your computer. When it's connected, your computer will start transferring your photos to your computer.

Insert one end of the cable into your camera and the other into your computer to begin the transfer.

Wifi or bluetooth

If your camera has wifi or bluetooth functionality, it should have a wifi or bluetooth icon. If your computer also has wifi or bluetooth you can move your photos from your camera to your computer without physically connecting the 2 devices.

Look at your camera instructions to find out how wifi and bluetooth work for your camera.
