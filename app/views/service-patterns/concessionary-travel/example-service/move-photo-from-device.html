Get help with transferring photo

Email

When you have a photo of yourself on a mobile phone, tablet or laptop, transfer the photo to the device you're using for this application by sending yourself an email and attaching the photo file.

Some devices also have a 'share' function with sending by email as an option. Choosing this opens a new email with the photo automatically attached.

Transfer cable

A transfer cable is a lead which can connect your device to your computer. When it's connected, your computer will start transferring your photos to your computer.

Insert one end of the cable into your device and the other into your computer to begin the transfer.

Wifi or bluetooth

If your device has wifi or bluetooth functionality, it should have a wifi or bluetooth icon. If your computer also has wifi or bluetooth you can move your photos from your device to your computer without physically connecting the 2 devices.

Look at your device instructions to find out how wifi and bluetooth work for your device.
